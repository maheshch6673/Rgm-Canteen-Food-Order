Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how RGM College Canteen App (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects the information of users (“you”) who use our Platform.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information required to provide our services. This includes:

  • Student Name
  • Student Registration Number
  • Contact Number

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as bank details, card information, passwords, or biometric information.

Use of Information

The information collected is used only for:

  • Processing food orders
  • Identifying students for order delivery or pickup
  • Contacting users regarding their orders

We do not use user data for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share user personal information with any third parties, except when required by law or for order fulfillment within the college canteen.

Data Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. Access to user data is limited only to authorized personnel.

Cookies

Our Platform does not use cookies to track users or collect additional personal information.

Data Retention

User information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose of the order or to comply with legal requirements.

User Consent

By using the Platform, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us using the details provided on the Platform.
