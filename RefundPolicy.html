Cancellation & Refund Policy

This cancellation and refund policy outlines how you can cancel an order or seek a refund for a product or service purchased through the Platform.

Cancellations will only be considered if the request is made before the order is prepared. However, cancellation requests may not be entertained if the order has already been communicated to the seller/merchant listed on the Platform and the preparation process has started. In such cases, cancellation will not be allowed.

The Platform does not accept cancellation requests for perishable items such as food and beverages once the order is prepared. However, a refund or replacement may be considered if the user establishes that the quality of the food delivered was not satisfactory.

In case of receipt of damaged, spilled, or incorrect food items, please report the issue to our customer service team on the same day of delivery. The request will be reviewed after verification by the seller/merchant.

If you feel that the food item received is not as shown on the Platform or does not meet your expectations, you must notify customer support within the same day of receiving the order. The customer service team will review the complaint and take an appropriate decision.
